The Steam Deck

OLED is a refreshed version of the original Steam Deck, featuring a larger OLED display designed for gaming. It offers a vibrant HDR OLED display, runs many Steam games well, has excellent controls and improved haptics, and better battery life.

The OLED display is designed for gaming with striking contrast, brilliant clarity, and a larger picture, providing brighter colors, blacker blacks, and amazing motion rendition.

The device also offers better battery life and faster WiFi compared to the original Steam Deck. It is available in limited quantities in the U.S. and Canada as the Steam Deck OLED Limited Edition.

Steam Deck with LCD is the original version and is a great entry point for portable PC gaming, balancing power, ergonomics, and battery life.
